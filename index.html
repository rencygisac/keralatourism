About Kerala

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a state in the southern part of India. Known for its palm-lined beaches, backwaters, and network of canals, Kerala is a popular destination for tourists seeking natural beauty and tranquility. The state also boasts a rich cultural heritage, with traditional arts, music, and dance performances being integral parts of its vibrant culture. Kerala's cuisine, known for its use of coconut, spices, and seafood, is another major attraction for visitors.
